About Us

Why Choose Us

Feasibility Study & Rout Survey

To overcome every obstacle in transporting your consignment our special team conducts feasibility studies and prepares feasibility report that help us strategize the transportation process including maintenance of roads, reinforcements to weak bridges and extra pullers and pushers for difficult rain.

Containerized Cargo

We provide logistics services for all types of container movements on a door- to-door basis in India and across the globe. Be it 20ft, 40ft, High Cube or Flat Rack, we optimize the stowage of your cargo by utilizing the most suitable containers.

General & Break Bulk Cargo

We have ample experience in providing total solutions as a Break-Bulk Handler. Our well-trained staff is capable to deal with any challenge related to Break-Bulk Cargo and ensures seamless, reliable movement of your cargo from the vendor to the project site for consignments that are imported and exported. All these services are offered at very genuine costing

Logistics Warehousing

ARUN LOZICS & SHIPPING SOLUTIONS has a well furnished & hi-tech warehousing facility in almost every part of India for efficient logistics coordination. We ensure that the shipments we store in our warehouses are stored with utmost care, precision & safely delivered to the destination. storage, consolidation, repacking, documentation, labelling, invoicing, finished and spares inventory management, distribution and services support, cross talking are all part of our spectrum of services. We also handle all logistics activities for spare parts including repair and reverse logistics, replacement, re-export and scrapping.

Air Cargo & Railway

We are one of the leading firms engaged in providing Railway & Air Cargo Service to esteemed clients. Our experts ensure to provide thee services utilizing special air crafts that make sure to dispatch the goods at customers premises within the promised time frame. In addition to this, the offered services are executed as per the diverse requirements of respectable clients.
